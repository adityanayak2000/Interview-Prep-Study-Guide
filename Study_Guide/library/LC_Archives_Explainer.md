# 📦 LC archives explained
_Company Drive folders in the Interview Prep library are practice packs — not online assessments._

## Why
§8 / “Company Wise LC Qs” folders collect **LeetCode-style / tagged practice**. Mislabeling them as “OA folders” confuses juniors and breaks repo rules.

## Correct labeling

| Wrong | Right |
|---|---|
| “Qualcomm OA folder” | “Qualcomm LC archive (legacy)” |
| Dumping 100 folders into every page | Optional one-liner on a company Resource Panel |

## How to use

1. Open a company file under [companies/](../companies/README.md).  
2. If the Resource Panel has an **LC archive (legacy)** Drive link, practice those problems.  
3. Full folder list lives in the library Context doc — do not paste every ID into indexes.
