## What this archetype optimizes for
- **DSA Medium–Hard** (arrays, trees, graphs, DP) over C pointer MCQ volume  
- **LLD / machine coding** (working small systems, not only UML)  
- **OOP + DBMS + CN** fundamentals  
- Light **HLD** for product sense
