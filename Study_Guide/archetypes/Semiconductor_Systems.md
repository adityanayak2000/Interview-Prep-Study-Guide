## What this archetype optimizes for
- **C/C++ semantics & pointer fluency** over LeetCode Hard volume.
- **OS + concurrency** as interview differentiators.
- **Linked lists** as the staple live-coding structure.
- Aptitude/MCQ OAs with negative marking (HirePro/CoCubes class).
- Light CN/DBMS except TI/BEL-style panels.

Product-company LLD/HLD and FAANG Hard DSA are **secondary** here (see Tier P/F later).
