## Process Overview
Most on-campus drives reported as **online test → Group Discussion → Technical → HR** (four stages). GD is a real differentiator vs many pure SWE product loops.

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform:** Unknown (Mercer Mettl sometimes assumed in India campus norms — not confirmed here).
- **Sections:** Code debugging (C/C++/Java) + aptitude/reasoning + core CS (CN/OS/DBMS/SQL) + ~2 easy–medium coding problems.
- **Duration / negative marking:** Unknown.

## Interview Rounds
- **GD:** ~6–8 candidates, ~30 min, current-affairs topics; panel scores communication/teamwork.
- **Technical:** Intro, deep project cross-questioning, DSA.
- **HR:** Fit and expectations.

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Practice GD communication explicitly — it gates technical for many drives.
- OA spreads weight across OS/DBMS/CN rather than DSA-only.
