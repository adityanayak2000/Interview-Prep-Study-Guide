## Process Overview
Patent analytics / IP strategy consulting (Gurugram HQ). Typical shape: **resume screen → online assessment → 1–3 technical/assessment rounds (resume-heavy) → HR** (± director for senior). A short video assignment appears in one GFG campus account and is not universal. US/Gurgaon Glassdoor variants use phone screen + take-home + multi technical.

_Caveat: medium / thinner than a typical services done-v3 — OA format differs by cycle; flag variance._

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform:** POD.ai named in one GFG Associate Consultant campus account.
- **Format A (GFG):** MCQs spanning aptitude, reasoning, programming/coding, networking-related items.
- **Format B (SVNIT ECE campus report, different cycle):** Technology-description prompts + aptitude + SWOT-style self-assessment + extracurricular questions.
- **Duration / negative marking:** Unknown.
- Unverified search-summary claims of dedicated “OOPS/DBMS/OS/Java” OA sections were **not** present in the fetched GFG text — excluded.

## Interview Rounds
- **Resume/project depth is the #1 signal** across GFG, SVNIT, and Glassdoor — interviewers open tech-industry / research threads from whatever you listed (telecom, semiconductors, ML, security, architecture, etc.).
- **Patent/IP content:** Primarily surfaced at more senior levels (patent understanding / reverse engineering on assessment) — light or absent for entry Associate Consultant accounts.
- **DSA:** Present in OA programming items; no detailed LeetCode-style live-bank found for fresher technical rounds.
- **HR:** Culture/communication/fit.

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Build deep project narratives — you will be cross-questioned, not quizzed from a standard DSA sheet.
- Expect OA format volatility; ask recruiters which assessment style your cycle uses.
- For Senior Consultant tracks, add patent/reverse-engineering literacy.
