# CBRE
**Role(s):** Software Engineer (campus/off-campus; confirm GWS/engineering vs other CBRE functions)  
**Tier:** G  
**Status:** done-v3

## Process Overview
Common SWE-leaning shape: **written/aptitude + CS MCQ screen → technical interview(s) with senior engineers → optional take-home/live coding (3–5 hr in some pipelines) → HR/culture**. CBRE spans many non-SWE businesses — confirm the India tech role before specializing.

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform / duration / marking:** Unknown.
- **Topics:** General aptitude, DBMS/SQL, CN, English commonly cited in MCQ screens.

## Interview Rounds
- DSA on arrays/strings/lists; SQL/REST; language questions (Java/Python/Go).
- Past projects discussed.
- Overall Glassdoor difficulty often around ~2.7/5 (moderate) — not a FAANG bar.

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Med | Low | Low | High | Med | Low | None | None | Med |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- SQL + medium DSA over deep system design for typical India SWE reports.
- Verify role family (tech vs facilities/GWS/non-engineering).
