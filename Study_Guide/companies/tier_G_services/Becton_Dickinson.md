# Becton Dickinson (BD)
**Role(s):** R&D Software (BD Technology Campus India, Bengaluru) — intern/campus and related SWE paths  
**Tier:** G  
**Status:** done-v3

## Process Overview
Reported shapes include **written MCQ (DSA/OOP/OS/aptitude/cognitive/English) → MS Teams technical (often 2 interviewers)** and alternate pipelines with HR screen → LeetCode-style coding → behavioral panel (~45 min/round; ~2 weeks end-to-end virtual).

## Online Assessment (OA)
- MCQ-based covering DSA, OOP, OS, aptitude, cognitive, English.
- Platform / duration / negative marking: Unknown.

## Interview Rounds
- Screening weight on OS + OOP fundamentals, not DSA alone.
- Live coding in technical rounds; resume discussion; manager culture-fit.
- No strong LLD/HLD/CN signal found for this fresher/internship-leaning sample.

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Med | Med | Low | Low | None | None | None | None | Med |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Balance OS/OOP revision with medium DSA.
- Expect a fully virtual multi-week calendar.
