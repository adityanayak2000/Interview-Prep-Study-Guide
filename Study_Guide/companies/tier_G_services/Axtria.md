## Process Overview
Recurring skeleton across campus and off-campus reports: **written/online test → technical interview(s) → HR/managerial**. Some role/year variants add a second technical with a hiring manager; Group Discussion is not found. Off-campus Data Analyst accounts name HirePro; older campus accounts name CoCubes.

_Caveat: medium confidence — OA section layout differs by year/role; do not force one template._

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform:** CoCubes (campus 2019 accounts) and HirePro (off-campus Data Analyst) both named; AMCAT/Mettl/HackerRank not found.
- **Duration:** ~80–90 minutes depending on source.
- **Sections:** Placement-syllabus sites commonly cite ~60 aptitude + ~20 technical (~80 total). GFG campus and off-campus write-ups use different partitions (company-knowledge subjectives + verbal/reasoning/quant/technical; or broader CS + DI + verbal batteries). Treat structure as **drive-dependent**.
- **Negative marking:** Explicitly none in at least one syllabus source; no contradiction found.
- **Cutoff:** One off-campus account cites ~60% correct to advance (single-source).
- **Difficulty:** Moderate.

## Interview Rounds
- **DSA:** Lists, trees (incl. LCA), graphs, strings, stacks/queues, greedy, DP — often MCQ-level or short problems rather than contest judges.
- **SQL:** Consistently heavy — joins, set ops, GROUP BY/COUNT, stored procedures; live table-to-query drills.
- **CS fundamentals:** OOP (incl. diamond problem), SDLC vs STLC, networking devices, basic OS/DBMS.
- **Python:** Collections differences, lambdas (esp. analytics tracks).
- **Projects:** Frequently “resume-based” technical rounds.
- **Puzzles/guesstimates:** Recurring (jar-labeling, ants-on-triangle, etc.).
- **HLD:** Only a weak single-source “system design basics” note for some deeper roles — unconfirmed.

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- SQL fluency is the clearest differentiator vs pure DSA product companies.
- Expect puzzles alongside CS fundamentals.
- Clarify SWE vs Analyst vs DE track before specializing prep (PySpark/Azure appear mainly on DE postings, not universal interview transcripts).
