## Process Overview
Aptitude/technical CBT (GradeMe historically) → multiple technical rounds (C/pointers/OS/DBMS/web basics) → HR / senior manager.

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform:** GradeMe reported in older campus drives (~60 min, ~50 Q).
- **Sections:** Aptitude; pseudocode; DBMS; testing MCQs.
- **Difficulty:** Easy–Medium.

## Interview Rounds
- SDLC/Agile, SQL, Java/C OOP, OS (semaphore, deadlock, VM).
- Coding: sorting, Fibonacci, patterns; nested SQL.
- Heavy pointer/dangling/void pointer grilling in some panels.
- Resume projects; puzzles.
