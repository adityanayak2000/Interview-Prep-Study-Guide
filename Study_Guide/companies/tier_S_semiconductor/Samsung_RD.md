# Samsung_RD
**Role(s):** Network Software Engineer / R&D (SRIB — Samsung R&D Institute Bangalore)  
**Tier:** S  
**Status:** done-v3

## Process Overview
Classic SRIB campus loop: brutal coding OA (often **all hidden TCs** / no STL on Samsung platform) → 2 technical interviews → HR. Graphs, DP, backtracking, and linked-list live coding recur.

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform:** CoCubes or Samsung proprietary (proctored); variants of 1 hard problem / 3 hrs **or** 2–3 problems / 90 min.
- **Rules:** Frequently **no STL**; limited submissions; must pass all/most hidden cases to shortlist.
- **Topics:** Graphs (cycle detection), DP/backtracking grids, arrays/strings.
- **Difficulty:** Hard elimination filter.

## Interview Rounds
- **Tech 1:** DSA live (LL cycle + length common); write runnable code.
- **Tech 2:** Projects + CS fundamentals (OOPs, OS, DBMS, CN) depth.
- **HR:** Why Samsung, role awareness, relocation.

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| High | Med | High | Med | Med | Low | Low | Low | Low |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Practice coding **without STL** (manual arrays, recursion) before SRIB OA season.
- Linked-list cycle ladder (hash → Floyd → length) appears in tech rounds.
- Networks developer roles still grill general DSA + projects.
