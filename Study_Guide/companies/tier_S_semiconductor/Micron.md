# Micron
**Role(s):** Firmware Engineer, Software / SSD-adjacent, manufacturing IT variants  
**Tier:** S  
**Status:** done-v3

## Process Overview
OA (Techgig / HirePro depending on drive) → 1–2 technical interviews → managerial/HR. Firmware tracks emphasize C, bit manipulation, OS, and COA; software tracks probe resume + core CS.

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform:** Techgig reported for some campus drives; HirePro for others (role-dependent).
- **Duration:** Often multi-section CBT (~60–90+ min class).
- **Sections:** Aptitude/logical; computer/programming (C output, strings, OS, CN, SQL, DSA); sometimes essay.
- **Topics tested:** C/C++, OS, aptitude, bit-level thinking for firmware paths.
- **Difficulty:** Medium; breadth over LeetCode-Hard.

## Interview Rounds
- Technical: resume/projects; OOPs; OS (thrashing, paging, semaphores, mutex, zombie); DBMS/SQL (Nth salary); bit manip / pointers for firmware.
- Managerial/HR: hobbies, Micron awareness, relocation, learning agility.

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Med | High | High | Med | Low | Low | None | Med | High |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Bit manipulation + C pointers are high leverage for firmware.
- Be ready to explain storage interest (SSD/NAND vocabulary) if applying firmware.
