# Western Digital / SanDisk
**Role(s):** Firmware Developer, Firmware Validation, software variants  
**Tier:** S  
**Status:** done-v3

## Process Overview
OA (~45–60 min, often Codility/similar with negative marking) → 3–5 rounds: technical (C/OS/COA/pointers), managerial, HR. Branding may appear as Western Digital or SanDisk by year.

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform:** Codility reported for some virtual drives; other campus CBTs vary.
- **Duration:** ~45–60 minutes common.
- **Sections:** Aptitude; C/C++ output; DSA; OS internals; electronics (for firmware-inclusive drives).
- **Negative marking:** Often yes (e.g. +1 / −0.25).
- **Difficulty:** Medium; speed matters (many MCQs).

## Interview Rounds
- Coding + puzzles; OS (virtual memory, semaphores); structure padding; bitwise tricks; dangling/volatile pointers; COA.
- Managerial: M.Tech motivation, teamwork, firmware/hardware interest (HDD/SSD).
- HR: short confirmation.

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Med | High | High | None | Low | Low | None | Med | High |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Treat C + OS + bit manip as the core stack.
- Show genuine storage/firmware interest in managerial rounds.
