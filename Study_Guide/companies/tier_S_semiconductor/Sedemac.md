## Process Overview
IIT/campus reports (IIT KGP Foresight etc.): Online assessment → 2–3 technical interviews (often process-over-answer, multi-domain: circuits, mechanics, aptitude, light coding) → HR. Engineering-dominated culture; unstable “MBA/US MS soon” answers hurt HR.

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform:** Campus OA (e.g. Nalanda/lab setups in IIT reports).
- **Focus:** Aptitude + domain (mech/EE/mechatronics) + coding for embedded SW tracks.
- **Difficulty:** Unusual breadth — not pure LeetCode.

## Interview Rounds
- **Tech 1–2:** Blank-paper problem solving (RC circuits, fluids/torque, probability, rough code); interviewers watch **process**. Long rounds (45–120+ min) reported.
- **HR:** Career stability, interest in hands-on engineering, no flip to UPSC/MBA narratives.
