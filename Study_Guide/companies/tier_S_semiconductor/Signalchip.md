## Process Overview
Campus-style process documented on GFG: online test with domain choice → multiple technical rounds (solution optimization, architecture, compilers) → HR/interest fit. Strong **hardware–CS hybrid** bar even for CS backgrounds.

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform:** Campus online test (details platform-agnostic in reports).
- **Sections:** Aptitude + choose **2 of 4** domains: Signal Processing, Digital Design, Analog Design, **C programming**.
- **Topics tested:** Selected domains + later rounds expand to COA/compilers.
- **Difficulty:** Medium–Hard; domain depth matters.

## Interview Rounds
- Explain and **optimize** written solutions.
- Resume/projects.
- Computer architecture: hyperthreading, thread types, memory hierarchy/cache opts, chip performance factors.
- Compiler design: tokens, stages, whether trees appear in lexing.
- HR: preferred work area; why Signalchip from CS.
