## Process Overview
PSU-style: written/GATE-like or aptitude+domain test → document verification → technical(+HR) panel. Strict punctuality and document checks. CS panels grill OS, DBMS, programming favorites + projects.

## Online Assessment (OA) / Written
- **Format:** Historically GATE-pattern departmental MCQs + aptitude (counts vary by year/notification).
- **Topics:** Core CS (OS, DBMS, DS) more than LeetCode Hard.
- **Difficulty:** Breadth exam discipline matters.

## Interview Rounds
- Document verification first (carry originals).
- Technical/HR combined panels: favorite subjects (thrashing, race conditions, 3NF, keys, ACID, DBMS vs RDBMS common in recent LinkedIn reports).
- Project explanation; why BEL vs IT services offers.

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Pick 2–3 subjects and go deep (OS + DBMS is a strong CS pair).
- Formal dress + paperwork readiness is part of the filter.
- Defense/electronics mission awareness helps HR “why BEL.”
