# Meesho
**Role(s):** Software Engineer (India campus)  
**Tier:** F  
**Status:** done-v3

## Process Overview
- Online coding assessment
- Technical/coding interview round(s) (DSA + development)
- System design round (SDE-2+, sometimes even SDE-1)
- HR/behavioral round

## Online Assessment (OA)
- Platform: HackerRank or HackerEarth (varies by drive). ~90-105 min (one report: 165 min). 2-3 coding Qs (medium-hard: trees/graphs/DP) + ~10 MCQs on OS/CN/SQL. Negative marking: one report cites +3/-3 for MCQs.

## Interview Rounds
- Round 1 (OA): DSA coding + CS-fundamentals MCQs
- Round 2: DSA problem-solving, sometimes SQL queries, resume/project discussion
- Round 3: System design (even for some SDE-1)
- Round 4: HR — behavioral, culture fit
- Senior SDE variant: machine coding (~2 hrs) + system design (~1.5 hrs)

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| High | Med | Low | Med | Med | Med | Med | None | Low |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Grind medium-hard DSA, especially trees/graphs/DP
- Brush up SQL query-writing and OS/CN basics for MCQ rounds
- Prepare basic machine coding/LLD even for SDE-1
- Practice explaining trade-offs aloud — clear articulation valued
