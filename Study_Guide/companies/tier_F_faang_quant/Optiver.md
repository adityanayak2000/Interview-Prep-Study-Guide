## Process Overview
- Online assessment (OA)
- HR/behavioral round
- 2-3 technical interviews (often same-day): coding + quant/math + market-making scenarios

## Online Assessment (OA)
- Platform: HackerRank-style (unconfirmed). Duration varies (80 min-3 hrs reported). 2 coding Qs + probability/mental-math/"games" questions. Negative marking unknown. Difficulty: efficient (non-brute-force) solutions required under time pressure.

## Interview Rounds
- Technical (SWE track): algorithmic coding (DSA + complexity analysis), computer architecture, networking, concurrency, memory management
- Quant-flavored: market-making scenario design, Black-Scholes derivation/implementation, dice/card-game market-making simulations testing probability reasoning
- Behavioral/HR: fit/motivation, reasoning transparency on design decisions

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Practice mental math/probability puzzles under strict time pressure — core, non-negotiable skill
- Grind efficient DSA (non-brute-force) since coding Qs are auto-scored on efficiency
- Review options-pricing basics (Black-Scholes), be ready to re-derive
- Prepare to justify design decisions out loud — reasoning transparency matters
- Brush up OS/networking/concurrency fundamentals for SWE-specific rounds
