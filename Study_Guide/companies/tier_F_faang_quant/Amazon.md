## Process Overview
Common fresher path: **OA (MCQ/coding + Work Style Assessment) → telephonic/technical → 2–4 F2F loops** (~5–6 rounds total). **Leadership Principles** are woven through essentially every round (STAR stories), not parked only at HR — the defining differentiator vs Google/Microsoft fresher loops.

## Online Assessment (OA)
- Aptitude/MCQ (C/C++, OS, DS, OOP) + coding (often 3–4 medium array/string/matrix problems) + separate ~20-min Work Style Assessment (LP-aligned sliders, ~50 items).
- Platform / negative marking: Unknown in the summaries used here.

## Interview Rounds
- DSA medium consistent across phone and F2F (arrays/trees/DP/lists/matrix/BST) — less of a late-round Hard cliff than Google.
- CS fundamentals moderate; OOP/DB appear on some tracks.
- System design minimal/absent for fresher SDE-1; relevant from SDE-2+.
- LPs assessed continuously with STAR — weight comparable to technical.

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Med | Low | Low | Low | None | Low | None | None | Med |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Build STAR stories mapped to Leadership Principles before grinding extra Hard DSA.
- Do not skip Work Style Assessment prep — it is a graded OA component.
- Keep medium DSA fluency consistent rather than only contest Hard.
