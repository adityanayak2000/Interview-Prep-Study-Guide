## Process Overview
Leaner than Google/Amazon for many campus reports: **OA (hard gate) → 2 technical interviews (± HR)**. Most eliminations happen at OA. Later rounds blend DSA with resume/project discussion and communication.

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Duration:** Often ~75 minutes.
- **Format:** 2 DSA questions (easy–medium bar; different sets per candidate).
- **Platform / negative marking:** Unknown in the sourced summaries used here.

## Interview Rounds
- DSA medium (array-heavy — rotations, DP variants); softer ceiling than Google/Media.net Hard bars.
- CS fundamentals present but secondary to problem-solving process.
- System design largely absent at fresher campus SDE level.
- Culture/fit usually woven into technical conversations rather than a separate formal round.

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Train specifically for timed medium OA pairs.
- Narrate approach clearly — communication is scored.
- Skip heavy HLD prep for fresher campus unless JD says otherwise.
