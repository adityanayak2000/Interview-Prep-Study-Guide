# Google
**Role(s):** Software Engineer (India campus / early career)  
**Tier:** F  
**Status:** done-v3

## Process Overview
Typical India fresher shape: **online coding OA → 2–4 technical/DSA rounds → Googleyness/behavioral**, often ~4–5 rounds over roughly a month. No separate aptitude battery at fresher level in the common accounts — process is DSA/CS-fundamentals driven. System design is light/occasional for freshers, not a universal fixed round.

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Format:** Online coding, often ~60 minutes, 2 DSA/CP-style problems (arrays/sorting/hashing common). Discuss brute force → optimal; code in Java/C++.
- **Proctoring / platform / negative marking:** Vary by cycle; not fixed in the sourced summaries used here.

## Interview Rounds
- **DSA bar:** High — medium-to-hard with optimal complexity and clear articulation.
- **CS fundamentals:** OS processes/threads, DBMS/SQL, CN, OOP appear in later rounds.
- **Googleyness & Leadership:** Dedicated behavioral assessing collaboration, ambiguity handling, attitude — not only scripted STAR checkboxes.
- **System design:** Occasional/light for fresher SWE.

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| High | Med | Med | Med | Med | Low | Low | Med | None |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Prioritize arrays/trees/graphs/DP with timed practice.
- Prepare genuine collaboration/ambiguity stories for Googleyness.
- Keep OS/DBMS/CN overlays warm, not only pure LC grind.
