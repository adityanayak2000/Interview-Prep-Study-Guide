## Process Overview
- Online Assessment (aptitude portal, mixed sections)
- Technical interview 1 — resume, OOP, DSA
- Technical interview 2 — DSA + low-level design (e.g. parking lot)
- Additional technical round for lateral hires (SQL, CS fundamentals)
- HR/behavioral round; ~1 month total

## Online Assessment (OA)
- Platform: AMCAT/Aspiring-Minds-style historically, HackerRank also mentioned. ~60-80 min combined; sections: aptitude (10 Q/20 min), debugging (~7 Q), coding (3 Q/60 min). Negative marking unknown. Difficulty easy-medium.

## Interview Rounds
- Technical: OOP (inheritance, overriding), DSA (trees, sorting, hashing, XOR subarrays, linked list deletion)
- LLD: parking lot, food-delivery-style (Zomato-like) design, RPC/HTTP basics
- CS fundamentals: OS (virtual memory, paging), DBMS, SQL (LeetCode SQL-style)
- Some tracks: math/stats (probability, linear algebra) — unconfirmed consistency
- HR: "why Morgan Stanley," career goals

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| High | Med | Low | Med | Low | Med | Low | None | Med |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Revise OS/DBMS — more emphasis here than at pure-DSA shops
- Practice 1-2 classic LLD problems (parking lot, elevator, food delivery) with class diagrams
- Brush up SQL query-writing (LeetCode SQL specifically cited)
- Prepare OOP fundamentals deeply — commonly opens technical rounds
