# Deloitte
**Role(s):** Analyst / USI tech (note: HashedIn-by-Deloitte is a separate product track)  
**Tier:** F  
**Status:** done-v3

_Caveat: **HashedIn by Deloitte** is a distinct product-engineering sub-brand with its own harder DSA loop — do not assume USI Analyst == HashedIn._

## Process Overview
- National Level Assessment (NLA) — combined aptitude + coding OA
- Versant test (spoken English, India-specific)
- Technical interview round(s) — 1-2 rounds
- HR/managerial round
- HashedIn/SWE track: ~3 rounds — 2 technical (fundamentals + architecture/scenario) + 1 shorter partner round

## Online Assessment (OA)
- Platform: Deloitte's own NLA platform. ~90 min-3 hrs. 5 timed sections: Verbal (13 Q), Reasoning (12 Q), Quant (10 Q), Coding (2 Qs, ~25 min); each section has a 75% cutoff. Negative marking unknown. Coding medium (anagram, majority element style).

## Interview Rounds
- OA (NLA): aptitude sections + 2 coding problems, per-section cutoffs
- Versant: English/communication screening (India-only)
- Technical: resume/projects, tech-stack choices, DBMS/SQL, OOPs, scenario-based team-difficulty questions
- HR/Managerial: career goals, culture fit

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Don't neglect the Versant English test — separate gate from technical rounds
- Per-section cutoffs in OA mean you can't skip a weak section
- Review resume thoroughly — interviewers cross-question tech choices
- If targeting HashedIn/engineering-heavy roles, prep DSA and basic system design separately (different bar)
