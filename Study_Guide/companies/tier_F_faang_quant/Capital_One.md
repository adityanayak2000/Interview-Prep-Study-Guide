## Process Overview
- Online assessment (CodeSignal GCA)
- Technical interview(s) — sometimes project/resume deep-dive rather than pure coding (India campus)
- Behavioral/HR round
- General (US-influenced) process: recruiter screen → HM screen → OA → "Power Day" (2 technical + 1 behavioral + 1 case)

## Online Assessment (OA)
- Platform: CodeSignal (GCA), proctored, webcam required. ~70 min, 4 questions (~70% DSA, rest data manipulation/debugging). Negative marking unknown. Difficulty: easy→hard progression; ~750+ score commonly cited to advance (US context, India threshold UNKNOWN).

## Interview Rounds
- Technical: at least one India on-campus case reported no coding — deep situational/behavioral discussion of resume projects/internships instead
- HR (~20-25 min): behavioral, PORs, extracurriculars
- Broader process: DSA, system design/case-style problems, STAR-format behavioral

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Practice LeetCode easy-medium (arrays, strings, hashmaps, trees) under strict OA timing
- Prepare a very thorough narrative of every resume project — expect deep drill-down
- Prepare standard STAR/behavioral answers with trade-off discussion
- Pace yourself in OA — later questions are harder and time-boxed
