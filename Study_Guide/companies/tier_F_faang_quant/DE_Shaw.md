## Process Overview
- On-campus: online round (aptitude + technical MCQs + 2 coding Qs), steep shortlist
- Technical interview rounds (2-3): DSA-heavy + CS fundamentals (OS/DBMS/OOP) grilling
- Off-campus/lateral: OA → multiple technical rounds incl. long first round (~4 hrs reported) + resume-grilling
- System design round for senior/SMTS-level candidates
- HR/culture-fit round

## Online Assessment (OA)
- On-campus: ~95 min, aptitude + technical MCQs + 2 coding problems, medium-hard. Off-campus: reported ~4 LeetCode medium-hard problems (separate pipeline). Platform/negative marking UNKNOWN for both.

## Interview Rounds
- Coding: DSA medium-hard (design insert/delete/get_random O(1), string/array problems); code must pass all test cases
- CS fundamentals deep-dive: OS (IPC, scheduling, memory, deadlocks), DBMS, OOP — real depth, not surface
- System design (senior tracks): search suggestion, slot booking, airline booking systems
- Resume-grilling round; HR/behavioral round

## Topic Weights

|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| High | High | Med | High | Low | Med | Low | Low | Med |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Be strong in both DSA/CP AND CS theory (OS/DBMS/OOP) — heavier fundamentals bar than typical FAANG
- Practice writing code that passes all judge test cases, not just logically correct pseudocode
- Don't neglect the on-campus OA's aptitude section
- Prepare a solid resume-grilling narrative for past projects
