# Reliance / Jio Platforms
**Role(s):** SWE (data primarily Jio Platforms, not RIL Retail broadly)  
**Tier:** F  
**Status:** done-v3

_Caveat: Public interview accounts are primarily **Jio Platforms Limited**, not Reliance Industries corporate / Retail broadly._

## Process Overview
- Pre-placement talk (campus drives)
- Online aptitude + coding round
- Technical interview (L1)
- Technical + HR interview (L2)
- Note: most public data is Jio Platforms Limited, not Reliance Industries corporate or Retail broadly

## Online Assessment (OA)
- Platform: InterviewBit reported in some drives (unconfirmed as consistent). Duration unknown. ~24 aptitude Qs (logical, quant, DBMS, verbal) + 2 coding Qs. Negative marking unknown. Difficulty: aptitude "tricky" DBMS/pseudocode; coding medium.

## Interview Rounds
- OA: aptitude (logical/quant/DBMS/verbal) + 2 coding problems (one string-based, one array/XOR-based reported)
- L1 technical: OOP concepts, basic system design, SQL/databases, sometimes job-specific stack
- L2 technical + HR: deeper technical + career goals/culture fit
- Reliance Retail (separate, thinner): DSA (Java-centric) + "architecture round" (scalability, microservices, DB design) — more system-design-flavored but lower confidence

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Med | Low | Low | High | None | Med | Low | None | High |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Practice medium DSA (arrays, strings, XOR/bit tricks appear in reported OAs)
- Revise DBMS deeply — described as a "tricky" aptitude-round section
- Prepare OOP fundamentals and basic system design/architecture discussion
- Confirm which Reliance entity (Jio Platforms vs Retail vs Industries corporate) a posting belongs to — pipelines differ
