## Process Overview
- Resume screening (academics, skills, projects)
- Online assessment/aptitude-technical test
- Technical interview (1-2 rounds, domain + coding/problem-solving)
- HR/final round (culture fit, career goals)

## Online Assessment (OA)
- Platform unknown. ~60 min (one variant). ~50 questions covering information management, OOP, networking, security, cloud computing, plus code-output-prediction (Python/NumPy/Pandas, Java, JS, some R). Negative marking unknown. Difficulty moderate, breadth-over-depth.

## Interview Rounds
- Aptitude/reasoning: logical, verbal, mathematical (distinct "career test" stage for freshers)
- Technical: banking-software/domain familiarity, general problem-solving, project discussion
- Behavioral: STAR-style teamwork/challenge questions
- HR: communication, attitude, career goals

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Prepare broad CS fundamentals (networking, security, cloud, DBMS) over deep algorithms
- Practice code-output-prediction (Python/Java/JS), not just from-scratch coding
- Prepare STAR-format behavioral answers — process leans behavioral/HR-heavy
- Know basic banking/domain context for technical-round discussions
