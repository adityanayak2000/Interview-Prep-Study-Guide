# Tata Steel
**Role(s):** Aspiring Engineer / Management Trainee tech track  
**Tier:** F  
**Status:** done-v3

## Process Overview
- Resume/CGPA shortlisting (off-campus AEP or on-campus MT drives)
- Written/online test (aptitude + technical + LR sections)
- Group Discussion (case-study based, some drive variants)
- Assessment round (~25 min, second-stage screen in AEP)
- Personal Interview (technical panel + HR)

## Online Assessment (OA)
- Platform unknown. ~1hr35min (AEP online test) + separate ~25min assessment in some pipelines. Negative marking unknown. Mixed MCQ+subjective (general maths, technical, logical reasoning; also behavioral/verbal/numerical in some drives). Not confirmed DSA-coding-judge style.

## Interview Rounds
- Written test: quant, technical MCQs, logical reasoning
- GD (case-study based) — some MT drives, not universal
- Technical PI: resume, projects, internships, domain/CS fundamentals (8-9 Qs), not always DSA-coding
- HR round: standard/basic; panel reported 3 interviewers (1 HR + 2 technical) in AEP track

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Low | Low | Low | Low | Low | None | None | None | High |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Prepare general aptitude (quant + LR) thoroughly — explicit gating round
- Practice case-study style GD if targeting MT tracks
- Be ready to discuss projects/internships in depth — PI is resume-driven
- Don't over-index on hard LeetCode-style DSA; core CS fundamentals + communication seem more decisive
