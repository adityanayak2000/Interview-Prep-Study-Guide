**Status:** done-v3

> 🚪 [Start](../../START_HERE.md) · 🗺️ [Map](../../README.md) · 🏢 [Companies](../README.md) · 📚 [Library](../../library/README.md) · 🔍 [Find](../../FIND.md)

## Process Overview
- Resume/recruiter screen
- 1 technical phone screen
- Onsite/virtual loop: varies — 3 rounds (coding, system design, HR) or fuller loop (4 behavioral + 2 technical + 1 system design)
- Team-specific technical deep-dive rounds (Apple hires per-team)
- Final HR/offer discussion

## Online Assessment (OA)
- Platform unknown; duration UNKNOWN (many teams skip OA, go straight to phone screens). Not consistently reported across sources.

## Interview Rounds
- Coding: DSA (merge sort, N-Queens, trapping rain water) + complexity analysis of given snippets
- SQL questions reported in at least one round
- System design: leaderboard design, SQL vs NoSQL, CAP theorem trade-offs, larger case studies (e.g. BookMyShow)
- Team-specific deep technical discussion, less pattern-matching
- HR/behavioral: motivation, past projects

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Practice explaining time/space complexity of arbitrary code, not just writing new code
- Brush up basic SQL query writing
- System design shows up even at SDE-1 level for some teams
- Tailor prep to the specific team/domain since Apple hires per-team
