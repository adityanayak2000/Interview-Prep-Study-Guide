# Mastercard
**Role(s):** Software Engineer (India)  
**Tier:** F  
**Status:** done-v3

## Process Overview
- Online coding round (1-2 questions)
- Technical Interview 1 — DSA + fundamentals
- Technical Interview 2 — practical/technical depth, sometimes design
- System design round for experienced hires
- HR round; "Super Day" for lateral hires (tech SME + hiring manager + bar-raiser)
- ~2-3 weeks average

## Online Assessment (OA)
- Platform unknown. ~1 hour, 2 coding questions. Negative marking unknown. Topics: strings (rotations, anagrams, missing elements). Difficulty easy-medium.

## Interview Rounds
- Technical 1: intro + DSA (anagram check, missing element in sequence)
- Technical 2: OOP (4 pillars with examples), DBMS (ACID), Java specifics (JUnit, Selenium, CI/CD), concurrency (mutexes/synchronization)
- System design (senior/lateral): scaling to millions, rollback, monolith vs microservices, sharding, consistent hashing
- HR round; bar-raiser round for lateral Super Day

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Med | Low | Low | Med | Low | Low | Med | Med | Low |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Entry-level: practice basic-to-medium DSA (strings, arrays) — lower bar than GS/MS but still required
- Prepare OOP with concrete real-world examples, not just definitions
- For experienced/lateral roles, revise HLD (sharding, consistent hashing, microservices)
- Know concurrency basics (mutex/synchronization) and testing tools if Java-stack role
