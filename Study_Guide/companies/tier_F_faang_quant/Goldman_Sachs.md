## Process Overview
- Online Assessment (HackerRank) — DSA-focused
- Virtual technical/coderpad round(s) — 1-2 live coding rounds
- "Super Day" — 3 back-to-back interviews same day (experienced/off-campus)
- Resume-based round covering projects, SQL
- HR/culture-fit round
- Timeline ~1-2 months end-to-end

## Online Assessment (OA)
- Platform: HackerRank. Duration ~90 min. 2 coding questions (easy/medium mix). Negative marking unknown. Difficulty easy-medium, occasional hard in later rounds.

## Interview Rounds
- Coderpad: 2 LeetCode-medium DSA problems (sorted array positions, islands, longest substring)
- Super Day: DSA (top-K tallest buildings, graph problems), language/OOP questions
- Later rounds: less technical, more aspirations/team-fit discussion
- SQL/Excel questions in some non-pure-tech tracks

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Drill LeetCode-medium arrays/strings/graphs/trees — bulk of OA and technical rounds
- Prepare a confident "why Goldman" + project-walkthrough narrative
- Practice live coding on shared editors under time pressure
- Expect a long process (~1-2 months); stay patient between rounds
