## Process Overview

**How this differs from Flipkart SDE:** The SDE track is built around a proctored **DSA online assessment** (often SHL, ~90 minutes, 2–3 coding problems), a signature **machine-coding** round, and DSA-heavy technical interviews. The **Data Scientist** track documented by Flipkart Careers does **not** describe that OA or machine-coding path. Instead it is **CV-driven**, then recruiter/HM screening, a technical phone screen, and multi-hour **presentation + hands-on modeling + depth rounds** on ML math and e-commerce case studies. Treat **ML Engineer** as a related but distinct lane: secondary reports often mention SQL/statistics screening and some DSA, closer to SDE than to the official DS loop — confirm the exact track on your invite.

**Off-campus / experienced (best documented):** Apply via careers page, referral, or recruiter outreach → exploratory call with hiring manager (optional, ~30 min) → technical phone screen with a peer (~60 min, resume depth + role-relevant problem solving) → virtual “onsite” block (often spread across days): presentation (if you have substantial prior work/research), 120-minute hands-on dataset modeling, 60-minute data-science depth case, 60-minute mathematical-modelling depth, 60-minute team/culture fit → offer. Round order and count vary by seniority; some candidates report condensed loops (e.g., three depth rounds + HM without a separate presentation).

**Campus (India, ME-relevant — thin public detail for core DS):** Flipkart recruits at major IITs and other engineering campuses for data-science-adjacent roles. Press coverage confirms **Data Scientist** hiring on IIT campuses, but published **step-by-step DS FTE** write-ups are scarce compared with SDE/GRID. The clearest **IIT campus** public accounts in 2024 are for **Business Analyst** (CV shortlist → technical interview on CV, probability/statistics, case study, guesstimate → director/strategy round) — **no DSA OA or machine coding**, aligning with the non-SDE pattern above. One off-campus Business Analyst candidate reported that an **on-campus** path would have included an assessment covering logical reasoning, statistics, SQL, and problem solving — still **not** the SDE SHL coding OA. **Do not assume** campus DS mirrors SDE OA without a role-specific invite.

## Online Assessment (OA)

**Data Scientist (official Flipkart Careers handbook):** No standalone timed coding OA is described. Screening is **CV shortlist → exploratory discussion → technical phone screen** before the hands-on and depth rounds.

**Machine Learning Engineer / some analytics campus paths (secondary, role-dependent):** Candidate guides and one Business Analyst experience suggest a **pre-interview assessment** may appear for ML/analytics tracks — topics cited include **SQL, statistics, logical reasoning**, and sometimes coding — but **platform, duration, and whether it applies to campus DS FTE are not consistently published**. No verified public ME-campus DS FTE OA pattern equivalent to SDE’s SHL 3-problem test.

**vs SDE:** SDE campus/off-campus commonly uses a **90-minute SHL (or similar) DSA OA** as the first gate; DS does not use that as the documented primary filter.

## Interview Rounds

| Phase | Round | Typical focus | Duration (reported) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-onsite | Exploratory / recruiter + HM | Role charter, CV walk-through, motivations; optional HM on call | ~30 min |
| Pre-onsite | Technical phone screen | Past projects in depth, role eligibility, DS/ML problem-solving | ~60 min (multiple screens possible) |
| Onsite (virtual) | Presentation | Past research or significant project; title/abstract shared in advance; open to Flipkart DS audience | ~60 min (45 + 15 Q&A) |
| Onsite | Hands-on skills | Dataset + problem statement; EDA, modeling, defend choices; internet allowed for docs; submit code; standard ML libraries only | ~120 min |
| Onsite | Depth in data science | Fuzzy e-commerce case; whiteboard end-to-end pipeline (data, features, models, metrics, experimentation); clarifying questions expected | ~60 min |
| Onsite | Depth in mathematical modelling | Deep dive on ML/DL math in an area you choose (often tied to presentation); derive/update formulations when business constraints change | ~60 min |
| Final | Team fitment / HM | Projects end-to-end, offline/online metrics, challenges, culture and stakeholder alignment | ~60 min |

**Campus-adjacent patterns (Business Analyst / analytics, not full DS loop):** CV shortlist → single technical on CV + stats + product case + guesstimate → senior/director case on Flipkart business model. Overlaps DS on **stats and product thinking**, lighter on **hands-on modeling block** and **math-modelling depth**.

**Recurring themes in candidate reports:** recommendation/search ranking, supply chain, fraud, A/B testing, metric choice for e-commerce, root-cause analysis, guesstimates on operational scale.

## Topic Weights

| DSA | ML / DL | Stats / Prob | SQL | Python / R | Case / Product | Math modelling | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Low–Med (ML Eng may be Med) | High | High | Med–High | High | High | High | Low–Med (analytics campus screen) |

**ML / stats emphasis:** bias–variance, cross-validation, train/test and experimentation design, classification/regression metrics, collaborative filtering, tree/ensemble methods, NLP/CV depth for senior profiles, hypothesis testing, Bayesian ideas, A/B testing, guesstimates. **SQL:** joins, window functions, CTEs, aggregations — especially analytics/ML Eng screens. **Coding:** Python-centric pipelines in hands-on round; not LC-style DSA grind as primary bar for DS.

## Recurring Patterns & Tips

- **Lead with your CV:** Internships and projects steer the entire loop; expect deep follow-ups on models you claim and metrics you shipped.
- **Prepare two modes:** (1) **live modeling** on a fresh dataset with narrated EDA and defensible model choice; (2) **whiteboard product cases** with explicit data, feature, model, offline/online metric, and rollout plan.
- **Math depth is non-optional:** Be ready to go from logistic regression through ensembles to neural architectures **from first principles**, not library API recall.
- **Presentation round:** Only for strong prior work; pick one project you can teach and defend under senior DS audience Q&A.
- **Do not prepare like SDE:** Machine coding and hard DSA OA patterns are the wrong default unless your offer letter explicitly says ML Engineer or SDE.
- **Separate tracks mentally:** Business Analyst / Data Analyst campus roles stress SQL, Excel, dashboards, and cases — overlap with DS interviews but typically skip the 120-minute modeling lab and formal math-modelling round.
- **Flipkart context:** Read their data-science mission (systemic intelligence across marketplace, supply chain, ads, search); tie answers to measurable business outcomes.
