## Process Overview
Société Générale **Global Solution Centre (SG GSC)** India (Bengaluru primary, Chennai) runs a consistent banking-IT SWE pipeline: **HirePro OA → technical(s) → hiring manager → HR**. Official young-graduates FAQ confirms a **4-round elimination** shape; campus reports often show **two technicals**.

| Path | Entry | Typical flow |
|---|---|---|
| Campus FTE / Intern+FTE | TPO / `socgenindia.hirepro.in` | HirePro OA → Tech (± Tech 2) → HM/Managerial → HR |
| Off-campus | careers.societegenerale.com / LinkedIn | Similar OA + 2–3 tech + HR; less consistently documented |

**Campus vs off-campus:** Same HirePro-style OA + fundamentals-heavy interviews; campus adds batch funnel and shift/location HR themes.

**M.E./M.Tech CS:** Official FAQ lists **≥6.0 CGPA / 60%** including **PG if applicable**, no backlogs. Same SWE pipeline as UG — expect deeper project/thesis grilling. Avoid advertising immediate higher-studies exit plans in HR (campus tip).

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform:** **HirePro** (majority of GFG campus reports). HackerRank in third-party guides is **not** corroborated for India campus.
- **Duration:** ~**90 minutes** common (section timers; unused time may not carry forward)
- **Sections:** Aptitude/reasoning · English/verbal · Technical MCQs · **2–3 coding**
- **Negative marking:** **No MCQ negative marks** in one 2020 HirePro report; otherwise Unknown for recent drives
- **MCQ topics:** OS, DSA, DBMS/SQL, OOP, SE/testing; sometimes CN
- **Coding topics:** arrays/strings, matrices, graphs, DP, hashing, LL; optimization scored
- **Difficulty:** Mostly easy–medium; some drives medium–hard

## Interview Rounds
1. **OA** — HirePro gate
2. **Technical 1 (~45–75 min)** — resume; DSA live/pseudocode; OOP (C++/Java); OS basics
3. **Technical 2 / Managerial (~40–60 min)** — projects; SQL/DBMS; puzzles; banking consistency themes; LLD/HLD if resume claims SD
4. **HR (~20–30 min)** — why SocGen; **shift flexibility**; Bengaluru vs Chennai; teamwork scenarios

**Note:** Reaching HR does not always guarantee offer.

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- **Fundamentals > trick DSA** — OS, OOP, DBMS appear as often as medium coding.
- Optimize OA solutions; completing all codes correlates with shortlist.
- Resume is the agenda — defend every stack bullet.
- Prepare shift/location answers; research GSC beyond generic bank lines.
- HR scenarios are elimination-grade — prep STAR stories.
