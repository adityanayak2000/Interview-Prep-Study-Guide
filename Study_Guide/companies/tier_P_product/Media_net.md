# Media.net
**Role(s):** SDE-1 / SDE (campus; Mumbai ad-tech)  
**Tier:** P  
**Status:** done-v3

## Process Overview
Highly selective campus loop: **proctored offline OA at college → 2 algorithm/technical interviews → managerial**. Roughly 3–4 rounds; funnels can shortlist only a tiny fraction past R2. Reputation across independent accounts is **Google/CP-hard**, not average campus DSA. Behavioral/LP culture stages are minimal vs Amazon — managerial is lighter resume/fit.

_Caveat: med-high confidence on DSA bar; platform/timing details still vary by batch._

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Format:** Proctored offline, ~1.5–2 hours; commonly 3 coding problems (some batches cite 5: easy/medium/hard mix). Topics span arrays, DP, graphs, bit manipulation; some drives also append ~45 aptitude/reasoning MCQs.
- **Negative marking:** Unknown.
- **Difficulty:** Medium–hard competitive programming.

## Interview Rounds
- **DSA bar high:** LC Hard-style / CP topics (greedy, DFS, DP, bit manipulation, segment trees) with full approach + pseudocode expected even early.
- **CS fundamentals:** Secondary but real (DBMS/OS/OOP/CN comparisons).
- **HLD/LLD:** Not evidenced as standard for fresher SDE-1.
- **Managerial:** Resume/discussion; no distinct heavy culture-fit round found.

## Topic Weights
| DSA | OS | C/C++ | DBMS | CN | LLD | HLD | Concurrency | Aptitude |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| High | Med | Med | Med | Low | None | None | Low | Med |

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Prepare like a CP contestant (Hard + advanced DS), not a services OA.
- Glassdoor difficulty often ~4/5 — prioritize algorithms over behavioral scripting.
- Expect brutally selective shortlists after OA.
