# Data Nimbus
**Role(s):** Data Engineer / Junior Data Engineer / Junior Technical Engineer (campus); Software Engineer / Technical Engineer (general); senior DE/ML/architect roles off-campus
**Tier:** P
**Status:** done-v3

## Process Overview
DataNimbus (data platform / Databricks-adjacent engineering) hires small campus cohorts in India (Hyderabad / Bengaluru / Chennai). Public process detail is strongest from **BITS Hyderabad Placement Chronicles** and college CDC circulars — not GFG/LeetCode Discuss.

| Path | Entry | Typical flow |
|---|---|---|
| Campus | TPO / CDC | Online coding test → technical (Python + SQL + basic DSA) → HR |
| Off-campus careers | datanimbus.com/careers | Mostly experienced roles; no sourced fresher OA |

**Campus vs off-campus:** Campus is a short **3-round** fundamentals loop. Careers portal skews **5–10+ YOE**. Do not confuse with unrelated “Nimbus Technologies” pages.

**M.E./M.Tech CS:** CBIT Jan 2022 circular listed **BE/M.Tech/MCA — CSE, IT, MCA**. BITS chronicle listed B.E. (all branches) for Data Engineer — treat M.Tech as **drive-dependent**.

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform:** Unknown (circulars say “Online Exam”; no HackerRank attribution)
- **Duration:** Unknown
- **Sections:** Coding-focused — BITS chronicle: **7 questions** across topics including **SQL + Python**
- **Negative marking:** Unknown
- **Topics:** Python, SQL, DSA; placed students also prepped Java/C++
- **Difficulty:** Tech round described as **basic** live Python/SQL + binary-search style DSA; OA difficulty not separately rated

## Interview Rounds
1. **Online coding test** — multi-problem screen (7 Q at BITS; funnel counts at SJB/CBIT)
2. **Technical** — live **Python + SQL**; basic DSA (e.g. binary search)
3. **HR** — behavioral; honest, direct answers emphasized

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- **Python + SQL** are the core bar — practice live coding, not only theory.
- DSA is present but **not** a FAANG-hard grind in campus reports.
- Small cohort hiring — fundamentals + clear communication matter.
- Post-join stack signals Databricks/Spark/ETL; certs appear as post-hire growth, not required interview proof.
- Campus title may be **Data Engineer**, not “SDE,” while work remains engineering on the data platform.
