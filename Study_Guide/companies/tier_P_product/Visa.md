## Process Overview
**Campus NCG:** Resume/CGPA shortlist → **CodeSignal OA** → PPT → same-day virtual interviews (typically **2 technical + 1 lead/manager**) on Teams + CodeSignal live coding.

**Off-campus (0–2 YOE):** Application → CodeSignal or HackerRank OA → 2–3 technical → HM/behavioral. Timeline often 1–3 weeks campus shortlists; 2–8 weeks off-campus.

**M.E./M.Tech CS:** No separate PG track in public reports — same pipeline as B.Tech NCG plus deeper resume/project grilling. Solving OA fully does not guarantee an interview slot (tight shortlists).

## Online Assessment (OA)
- **Platform:** CodeSignal (dominant in campus NCG 2024–2025); HackerRank also cited
- **Duration:** ~70 min (campus NCG common); ~90 min in some reports
- **Format:** Usually **4 coding problems** (easy→hard ramp); some 3-problem HackerRank loops
- **Topics:** strings/arrays, grids/BFS, heaps, query counting, graphs, DP, sliding window
- **Negative marking:** Not reported
- **Advancement:** Multiple reports suggest ~3/4 fully correct can pass; slots still scarce

## Interview Rounds
1. **Technical 1 (30–60 min)** — 1 DSA + CS fundamentals (OOP, OS: deadlock, thrashing) + project deep-dive
2. **Technical 2 (60–70 min)** — Resume-driven: auth/JWT, Spring Boot, caching/LRU, SQL + API/schema design, more DSA
3. **Lead / manager (~30 min)** — behavioral, why Visa, light system/module discussion
4. Off-campus may add explicit **system design** (URL shortener–style)

## Recurring Patterns & Tips
- Practice timed 4-problem CodeSignal-style sets.
- Thought process > perfect first code.
- Resume is the interview agenda — own every stack bullet.
- Fundamentals (OS, OOP, SQL, caching) appear as often as medium DSA.
- Same-day stamina for campus loops.
