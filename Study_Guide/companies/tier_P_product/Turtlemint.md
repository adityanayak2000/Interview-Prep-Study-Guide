## Process Overview
Turtlemint (insurtech; Mumbai / Pune / Goa) has **thin public SDE campus documentation**. Best signals: AmbitionBox (few SE/SD reports), JDs on Cutshort/Startup Goa, and intern→FTE profiles. GFG has no dedicated SDE interview-experience article; LeetCode Discuss / Glassdoor / TheJobOverflow empty for process detail.

| Path | Entry | Typical flow |
|---|---|---|
| Off-campus / experienced | careers@turtlemint.com, Cutshort, LinkedIn | Resume → coding/assessment → technical(s) → HR; AmbitionBox FAQ under ~2 weeks |
| Intern → FTE | Campus / apply | ~6-month intern → SWE return offer (profile pattern) |
| Campus engineering | Occasional visitor (e.g. IIIT-B named) | Institute-level JAF → optional online test → interviews — **Turtlemint-specific rounds Unknown** |
| Campus sales | LinkedIn campus tours | Not an engineering SDE pipeline |

**M.E./M.Tech CS:** No BITS/ME SDE placement write-up. Expect **off-campus or intern** path more than a published campus SDE loop.
